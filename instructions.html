Instructions

  • When you start the quiz, you will see an expression in informal English. Your task is to choose the meaning of it from four options (A,B,C,D).
  • If the choice is correct, the background colour will change to green and you'll get a point.
  • If the choice is wrong, the background colour will change to red and you won't get a point.
  • There are 10 questions and you get one point for each correct choice.
  • The maximum score you can get is 10/10.
  • If you want to save your score, enter your name in the submit form at the end of the quiz.
  • If you're not happy with your score you can retake the quiz to practise more. Enjoy!
